How does PeekSpeak work?

PeekSpeak uses advanced AI technology to help you practice pronunciation and speaking skills. Our app provides interactive lessons with real-time feedback to improve your language learning experience.

What languages are supported?

Currently, we support multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and more. We're constantly adding new languages based on user feedback.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved and synced across devices. You can view your streak, completed lessons, and achievements in your profile section.

What is PeekSpeak Pro?

PeekSpeak Pro offers unlimited access to all lessons, advanced AI features, personalized learning paths, and priority support. Upgrade to unlock your full learning potential!

Can I use PeekSpeak offline?

Some features work offline, but for the best experience and AI-powered feedback, an internet connection is recommended.
